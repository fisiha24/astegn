📚 About ASTEGN AI

ASTEGN AI (አስጠኝ AI) is a free AI-powered tutoring platform designed specifically for Ethiopian students and teachers. Our mission is to make quality education accessible to everyone, anywhere, at any time.

🎯 Our Mission

To build a strong educational foundation for students in Ethiopia from Kindergarten (KG) all the way to Grade 8 and beyond. We believe every student deserves access to personalized learning support.
